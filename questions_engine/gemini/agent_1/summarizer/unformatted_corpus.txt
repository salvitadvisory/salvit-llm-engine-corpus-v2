Overall Summary - 1-2 sentences that capture the overarching summary of the business that always begins with the business name using `<span class="coName">[CLIENT_ID]</span>`, and when paired with detailed, bullet-points (at least 2-3 bullets per domain), form the robust Executive Summary .
